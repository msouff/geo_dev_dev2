    Impounded Waters in Utah

    The purpose of this site is to show the locations of all the dams in the state of Utah. On the map a point is used to mark the location of each dam. The watershed along with the major rivers in the area around each dam are also displayed. When a dam location is clicked a pop up box will appear displaying the name of the selected dam. From the home page, the map can be reached through the map button located at the left of the screen or the button in the bottom right hand corner of the page.

    East Canyon Dam located in Morgan, Utah.

    Data Source

    All of the data that was gathered to make this site possible were found on the Utah AGRC website: gis.utah.gov
